Problem

One of the major challenges for online businesses is detecting the use of VPNs or proxy servers. Without this information, businesses cannot accurately identify and prevent fraudulent activities or ensure that their website is being accessed by legitimate users. This lack of information can lead to security risks and decreased customer trust.

Solution

VPN/Proxy Detection is a powerful solution that identifies whether a user is accessing a website through a VPN or proxy server. This technology enables businesses to prevent fraudulent activities, improve website security, and maintain the trust of their customers.

With VPN/Proxy Detection, businesses can accurately identify and block unauthorized access, ensuring that their website is being accessed by legitimate users. This feature also enables businesses to improve the accuracy of their website analytics and better understand their users' behavior. Overall, VPN/Proxy Detection provides businesses with valuable insights into their website traffic, allowing them to protect their website and customers from security risks.
